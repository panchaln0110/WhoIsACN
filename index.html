Who is ACN?

Your journey to success begins at ACN.

In 1993, four entrepreneurs stopped searching for the company they wanted - and created it.

Greg Provenzano, Robert Stevanovski, Tony Cupisz and Mike Cupisz parlayed decades of experience in direct sales to the creation of a company designed to support the enterprising spirit of dreaming and achieving far beyond industry confines.

ACN was born, not only as an answer to that calling, but also as a pathway to change lives for those willing to put the work into their own businesses.

The ACN Opportunity began with communications services of the day: long distance service and pagers (remember those?). ACN has since become a leader in the ever-evolving telecommunications and essential services industries.

Leading publications have recognized ACN’s stellar record and integrity, including:

Direct Selling News

Fortune magazine

SUCCESS From Home magazine

SUCCESS magazine

U.S. Business Review

USA Today Wall Street Journal

And many more!

What has ACN accomplished?

Within five years in business, ACN ranked 22nd in Inc. magazine’s list of fastest-growing companies in North America.

Within 25 years in business, ACN has become the world leader in direct sales of telecommunications, energy and essential services. It operates across North America, Latin America, Europe, the Pacific and Asia.

Co-Founders

Meet the ACN founders who made it all possible

These four men began ACN with a legal pad of paper with a line drawn down the middle. On one side, they listed elements of multi-level marketing that worked. On the other, parts of the business that did not.

They tore the sheet in half. The values listed became the bedrock for a company that has thrived in its first 25 years - and changed multitudes of lives along the way.

Core Values

Core values pave road to achievement at ACN

The ACN opportunity is nothing without the founding principles in place since Day 1. No matter the growth, no matter the rising potential, these principles will not be compromised.

Integrity

It's in policy and procedures. It's instilled in ACN Co-Founders, corporate level employees globally, Circle of Champions, Regional Vice Presidents and those newest to ACN.

Commitment to giving back

Every customer and bill they pay continues this mission, through ACN's Project Feeding Kids. ACN also supports Ronald McDonald House and local causes in our communities.

IBOs, customers above all

ACN's Co-Founders have always put the needs of IBOs and customers above their own with every decision. They're the lifeblood of ACN, and without them, nothing is possible.

ACN’s Integrity in Action

These core values guide ACN’s Co-Founders and Independent Business Owners to:

  • Operate with the utmost integrity
  • Adhere to the highest possible ethics standards
  • Perform under a marketing plan compliant with consumer protection laws

ACN’s top priority is to operate with credibility, honesty and integrity.
